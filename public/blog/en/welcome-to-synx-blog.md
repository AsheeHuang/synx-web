## Why We Built Synx

At Synx, we believe that **privacy and control** are fundamental rights when it comes to your financial data. Unlike traditional wealth management apps that require access to your bank accounts and send your data to remote servers, Synx takes a different approach:

### Privacy-First Architecture

Your financial data **never leaves your device**. Synx only connects to the internet to sync public market prices for stocks, cryptocurrencies, and precious metals. We don't ask for your bank passwords, we don't store your data on our servers, and we never sell your information to third parties.

### Complete Control

With Synx, you have full control over your financial information. Backup to your personal iCloud account, export manual backup files, and rest assured that your data stays with you even if you switch devices.

### Real-Time Insights

Track your net worth growth, asset allocation, and investment performance with interactive visualizations. Synx automatically updates market prices for your holdings across global markets, ensuring your portfolio value is always current.
