Have you ever tried entering a fund code in Synx, only to get no results? You're not alone — and it's not a problem with the app. It comes down to how funds are structured as an asset class. Let's explain why, and walk you through the best ways to find your fund in Synx.

---

## Why Don't Funds Have Standardized Codes?

Stocks have clear, universally recognized tickers — TSMC is 2330, Apple is AAPL — because they're listed on exchanges that assign and manage those codes. Enter a ticker on any platform, and you'll get the same result.

Funds are different. Each fund is issued independently by a fund company and sold through various channels — banks, brokerages, asset managers — without being listed on a single exchange. As a result, there's no global authority that standardizes fund codes. Different platforms and data sources may use different names or codes to refer to the exact same fund.

That's why searching for a fund by code isn't as straightforward as searching for a stock.

---

## Where Does Synx Get Its Fund Data?

Synx sources its fund data from the **Taiwan Open Government Data platform**. All fund codes in Synx come directly from that source, which means coverage is focused on funds registered in Taiwan — with complete, official data backing.

---

## How to Find Your Fund in Synx

Synx supports two ways to search for funds:

### Option 1: Search by Code

Since Synx uses codes from the Taiwan Open Government Data platform, you can look up your fund's code there first, then enter it in Synx:

- Domestic funds → [Domestic Fund Basic Data (sitca.org.tw)](https://data.gov.tw/dataset/43476)
- Offshore funds → [Offshore Fund Basic Data](https://data.gov.tw/dataset/11639)

These datasets also include the fund's English name, Chinese name, and **ISIN Code**.

### Option 2: Search by Name

If you know the fund's name, you can search directly in Synx by name. A few things to keep in mind:

- **Domestic funds**: Search using the **Traditional Chinese name**.
- **Offshore funds**: Search using the **English name**.
