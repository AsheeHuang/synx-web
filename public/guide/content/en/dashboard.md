Synx builds a dedicated analysis page for every single investment holding (such as individual stocks or cryptocurrencies), helping you dive deep into the performance of each specific asset.

---

## 1. How to Open the Dashboard

You can access the individual asset dashboard in two ways:

**Method A: Via Account Details**
Go to the specific investment account's detail page âž” Tap the **"More Info"** button in the top-right corner.

**Method B: Via Portfolio Page**
In the asset list table within the Portfolio tab, simply tap directly on the **Name** of the asset you wish to inspect.

---

## 2. Historical Analysis & Charts

Beyond current numbers, this is where you review your investment journey:

* **Historical Holdings:** View a complete log of all past buy/sell transactions and how your share count has changed over time.
* **Trend Charts:** Use interactive charts to visualize cost changes, market price trends, and the trajectory of your average cost. This helps you evaluate the timing and quality of your entry and exit points.

---

## 3. Financial Metrics

The dashboard calculates and displays key financial data for the asset in real-time:

* **Price:** Current market price.
* **Quantity:** Total shares or units held.
* **Realized P&L:** Profit or loss from sold positions.
* **Unrealized P&L:** Paper profit or loss on current holdings.
* **Avg Cost:** Your average buy-in price per share.
* **Total Cost:** Total capital invested.
* **Total Fees:** Transaction fees incurred.
* **Last Updated:** The timestamp of the latest price sync.
