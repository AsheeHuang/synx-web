If you have accounts that need to be tracked but should not be counted towards your personal total assets (e.g., funds managed for others, company money, or temporary transit accounts), Synx provides a feature to exclude them from your statistics.

---

## 1. How to Setup

Please follow these steps:

1.  From the **Insight** page, tap to enter the specific account.
2.  You are now on the **Account Details** page.
3.  Tap the **...** button in the top-left corner.
4.  Find and select **"Exclude from Stats"** in the menu.

---

## 2. The Effects

Once this feature is enabled, the system will stop including this account in your overall financial data. The specific effects are:

* **Group Total Exclusion:** The balance of this account will be deducted from the "Account Group" totals.
* **Chart Exclusion:** Data from this account will not appear in the "Net Worth Line Chart" or asset allocation diagrams.
